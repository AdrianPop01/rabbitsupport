  SPONSORS


  Sponsorship costs £15 per year per rabbit


  A photo and known details of the chosen rabbit are given to the sponsor as well as our three newsletters each year


  The Sponsor a rabbit initiative that we launched in 2004 continues to be the main source of income for our home. For just £15 a year you can sponsor a rabbit and receive a regular update letter from “your rabbit” as well as cards at Christmas and on your Birthday. It is an ideal gift for someone who is unable to have their own rabbit.


  150 people renewed their sponsorship last year and 50 people became new sponsors . The rabbits available for sponsor are our long term residents who we have not been able to rehome and now have become our permanent residents.

  Adopting a Rabbit


  For people looking to adopt we have a database of rabbits a list of rescue centres and offer advice on common pre-adoption questions such as what breed? One rabbit or two? And will it get on with my cat?


 Aims


  Although our main aim is to put potential adopters in contact with those currently caring for rabbits in need of homes. We also aim to:


* Raise awareness of the unwanted rabbits available in rescues
* Assist members of the public in finding homes for their unwanted pets
* Work towards solving the problems that cause rabbits to become unwanted
* Support the work of rescue centres


  Does it Work? Yes


  The Rabbit Rehome website receives approximately 42,000 visitors and 246,000 page view every month. Since its launch in September 2002 we have listed details of over 9000 rabbits available for adoption. We pride ourselves on finding homes for even the most difficult of rabbits. On several occasions a rabbit has been snapped up within hours of appearing on the site. Two in five of the rabbits listed find their new owner via an enquiry from Rabbit Rehome!
